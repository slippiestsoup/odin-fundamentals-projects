Chickpea Salad

This easy chickpea salad recipe is satisfying and delicious. Filled with protein-packed garbanzo beans, fresh vegetables, and a zesty lemon dressing, it's a perfect picnic side dish or healthy lunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, grated
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 3 cups cooked chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups mixed yellow and red grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 english cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup pickled red onions
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint, plus whole mint leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, mustard, salt, and several grinds of pepper.
  2. Add the chickpeas, tomatoes, cucumber, pickled onions, and olives, and toss to coat.
  3. Season to taste, garnish with resh mint leaves, and serve.
